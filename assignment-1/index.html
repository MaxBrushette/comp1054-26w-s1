About

I believe the song is about the anxiety of growing up and realizing that you aren't living up to your own expectations of where you yourself as a person should be at in life. I relate heavily to this song because I have struggled to become who I want to be for the entirety of my life, and I feel like there is no escape from the reality of who I am.

Tiger Really is a small 'jazzy and emo' band from Vancouver, BC, with around 12 thousand followers and 50 thousand monthly listeners on Spotify.
